Possibilities

Future Chapters

The same land. Five different futures.

The best estates are bought not for what exists — but for what could exist.

A buyer is unlikely to acquire 134 acres because of the house. They are far more likely to acquire it because they can see themselves building a future here. These are five of them.

Chapter One

A Family Estate

A place for generations to gather — long lunches on the veranda, children among the gardens, grandparents in the studio. Room enough for everyone, and quiet enough for each to find their own corner of the land.

Serra Luma becomes the family seat: a private world to return to, season after season, decade after decade.

Chapter Two

Wellness Retreat

Stillness is the rarest luxury. With creek frontage, native forest and a secluded plateau, Serra Luma offers the ingredients of a world-class wellness destination — yoga at sunrise, spa pavilions among the trees, and guests who leave changed.

The land already holds the silence. It awaits only the vision.

Chapter Three

Creative Sanctuary

For the writer, artist, musician or maker, few places inspire like this one. Light pours through timber and glass; the valley shifts with the hours; the studio stands ready as a private atelier or residency.

A retreat for one creative life — or a gathering place for many.

Chapter Four

Regenerative Holding

One hundred and thirty-four acres of forest, pasture and waterway is a legacy in waiting — for regenerative agriculture, rewilding, carbon and biodiversity stewardship, or a quiet life lived close to the land.

A holding to be tended, restored and passed on better than it was found.

Chapter Five

Eco Tourism Opportunity

Under an hour from Byron Bay and the Gold Coast, with established access and a cleared plateau, Serra Luma is rare canvas for low-impact, high-value eco accommodation — cabins among the canopy, a destination defined by nature and discretion.

The groundwork has begun. The opportunity is exceptional — subject to approvals.

Which future will you write?
